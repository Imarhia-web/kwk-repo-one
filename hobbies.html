What Am I Doing Now?

Most of my hobbies are either creative or really athletic. I love team sports and arts and crafts. My current hobbies are volleyball and ice skating with the occasional time for sketching, painting and knitting.
I really love a hands on creative activity that allows me to turn my brain off, those make me feel most like a kid.
One of my favourite quotes I think of when I decide to invest energy into something is from an all time favourite children's book. "All grown-ups were once children, but only few of them remember it" - The Little Prince.
